Development of a prototype to facilitate the mobility of blind people through object recognition and text to speech technology

Ignacio Sánchez López, Rodrigo García-Carmona, Abraham Otero
Universidad San Pablo CEU

Abstract

TagIt! is a prototype that enables blind people to get a better understand of their environment. It makes use of a camera, Wi-Fi connectivity and a speaker to capture images of the environment, process them looking for objects and describe such objects to the user using speech. It is presented in a wearable format that can be integrated in a standard white cane or worn.
